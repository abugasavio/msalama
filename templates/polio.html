The polio is a live attenuated virus. The Sabin type of vaccine is given orally, the one administered in Kenya. The other type is the Salk vaccine, used in other countries, and is administered via an injection. The vaccine is in four doses. Two drops in the mouth are recommended for each dose. Booster doses are sometimes given to children below five years of age in the country, regardless of their immunisation status

  • Polio 2 - 6 Weeks after birth or first contact
